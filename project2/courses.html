Full Time Makeup Courses

AOPM's full time makeup courses prepare students for the real world of working as a professional freelance makeup artist in the fashion, bridal, pictographic and special FX industry. Our London makeup courses, nail courses & hair styling courses are taught using different industry guest artists almost every day of the course so students will gain up-to-date tips and tricks from our International & London makeup artists currently working with the biggest names of the industry. Students who apply for our artistic pro, total pro & hair and makeup courses are invited to apply for our 700+ yearly work student work placements and a life time of 100s of FREE aftercare master classes. Our makeup courses are available in our London makeup school & New York makeup school.

Part Time Makeup Courses

AOPM's part time courses in London & New York prepare students for working as a top professional freelance makeup artist or hair stylists. Don't bother with 3-4 hour night classes when you can lean in the same way as a full time student but over the weekends. Our makeup courses, hair styling courses and bridal makeup courses are all on offer over a part time period in our London makeup school. Students who apply for our hair and makeup courses will be able to take advantage of our 700+ yearly student work placements and 100s of free aftercare classes.
