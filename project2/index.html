Academy of Professional Makeup
 

AOPM(Academy of Professional Makeup)i s proud to be a makeup school like no other. Since The Academy of Professional Makeup opened it’s doors as a Makeup School offering makeup courses more than a decade ago, we have placed our makeup school on more than 6000 assisting and work opportunities in London, Paris, New York, Dubai as well as our revolutionary Online Makeup School. We are a truly global makeup school and the ONLY makeup school in the world to take it’s graduates to assist and work backstage every year in all 4 fashion capitals of the world; London, New York, Paris and Milan.

 

Our Make Up School students take advantage of 100s of job placements and aftercare makeup course master classes with the industry’s leading specialists every year, as well as makeup discounts from the world’s most famous luxury and professional makeup brands. AOFM’s international makeup courses are taught by our team of over 40 handpicked freelance makeup artists from all over the world. Our makeup courses include makeup for fashion and photographic, bridal makeup and our famous creative Special FX classes. Once our makeup students have finished their courses they can choose from 100s of global work placements and assisting opportunities through our exclusive AOPM international bookings agency.
