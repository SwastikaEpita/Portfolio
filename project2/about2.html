About Professional Makeup Academy

  

Get your makeup certification from the world's leading online makeup school!

 

Benefit from unmatched professional online makeup artistry training

We’ve set the benchmark for excellence in distance education since 1984 and have many thousands of successful graduates. You could be one of them.
 

With Professional's online makeup artistry classes, you’ll receive professional training from industry professionals. Through video tutorials,celebrity makeup artist, will teach you makeup techniques step by step. You’ll also have a personal tutor to guide you through your course(s). Professional's provides even better training than what you’d receive at a brick and mortar school. You’ll benefit from regular one-on-one instruction and access to help whenever you need it.
